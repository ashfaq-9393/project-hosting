About Psychpal


This project leverages Federated Learning (FL) to develop a personalized mental health therapy chatbot while ensuring user privacy, personalization, and quick response times. Unlike traditional AI models that store user data on central servers, this chatbot learns from interactions directly on users' devices, sharing only encrypted model updates to improve global learning.



You can ask following


I’m feeling anxious. Can you help me calm down?
Give me some mindfulness exercises to reduce stress.
I had a rough day. Can we talk about it?
Suggest some positive affirmations for self-confidence.
I need breathing techniques to relax.
How can I improve my sleep quality?
Help me track my mood and emotions over time.
What are some tips to manage social anxiety?
I’m feeling Stressed. Can you guide me through a quick relaxation exercise?


Psychpal primarly focus on


Enhance privacy: Users conversations remain on their devices, with only model updates shared.

Improve personalization: The chatbot learns user-specific preferences and adapts responses accordingly.

Maintain scalability: By training on decentralized devices, the model continuously improves without centralized data storage.
